Cookies

👩‍🍳 Chef: Rahaf ⏱ 30 min ⭐ Beginner

Ingredients

2 cupsFlour
1 cupSugar
1/2 cupButter
1Egg
1 cupChocolate chips

About the Recipe

Learn how to bake soft and delicious cookies with simple steps.

Cookies are quick to make and perfect for beginners.

Tips

• Do not overmix the dough.

• Leave space between cookies while baking.

• Use room-temperature butter for best results.

• Do not overbake — remove when edges are golden.
