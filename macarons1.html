Macarons

👩‍🍳 Chef: Lama⏱ 60 min⭐ Advanced

Ingredients

1 cupAlmond flour
1 cupPowdered sugar
2Egg whites
1/4 cupGranulated sugar
1 tspVanilla

About the Recipe

This lesson teaches how to make delicate French macarons step by step.

Macarons require precision but give beautiful and tasty results.

Tips
