Cookies Preparation Steps

Follow these simple steps to prepare soft and delicious cookies.

1

Prepare Ingredients

Prepare all ingredients before starting.

2

Mix Butter & Sugar

Mix until smooth and creamy.

3

Add Eggs

Mix well after adding eggs.

4

Add Dry Ingredients

Add flour and chocolate chips.

5

Shape Cookies

Shape dough into small cookie portions.

6

Bake

Bake until golden brown.

Quick Notes

  • Do not overmix the dough.
  • Use room-temperature butter.
  • Leave space between cookies.
  • Do not overbake.
