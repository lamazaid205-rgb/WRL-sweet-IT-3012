Macarons Preparation Steps

Follow these simple steps to prepare colorful and delicate macarons.

1

Sift Dry Ingredients

Sift almond flour and powdered sugar together.

2

Whip Egg Whites

Whip egg whites with sugar until stiff peaks form.

3

Add Color

Add a small amount of food coloring if desired.

4

Fold Mixture

Gently fold dry ingredients into the egg white mixture.

5

Pipe Shells

Pipe small circles onto a baking tray.

6

Bake and Fill

Bake, cool, then fill with buttercream.

Quick Notes

  • Do not overfold the batter.
  • Rest shells before baking.
  • Use exact measurements.
  • Cool completely before filling.
