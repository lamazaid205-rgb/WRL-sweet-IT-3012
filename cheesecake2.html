Cheesecake Preparation Steps

Follow these simple steps to prepare a creamy and delicious cheesecake.

1

Crush Biscuits

Crush the biscuits into fine crumbs.

2

Prepare Base

Mix biscuit crumbs with melted butter and press into a pan.

3

Mix Filling

Mix cream cheese, sugar, eggs, and vanilla.

4

Pour Filling

Pour the filling over the biscuit base.

5

Bake

Bake until the filling is just set.

6

Chill

Let it cool, then chill before serving.

Quick Notes

  • Use soft cream cheese.
  • Do not overbeat the filling.
  • Cool before cutting.
  • Keep refrigerated.
