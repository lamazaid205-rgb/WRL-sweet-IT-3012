Cheesecake

👩‍🍳 Chef: Wjood⏱ 50 min⭐ Intermediate

Ingredients

200 gBiscuits
1/2 cupMelted butter
2 cupsCream cheese
1/2 cupSugar
2Eggs
1 tspVanilla

About the Recipe

This lesson teaches students how to make a creamy cheesecake with a crunchy biscuit base and a smooth filling.

Cheesecake is an elegant dessert and perfect for special occasions.

Tips

Use room-temperature cream cheese for a smoother filling.

Do not overbake the cheesecake.
